Conference Program

Program Overview

The program for The Web Conference 2026 will be available closer to the conference date. The conference will take place from April 20-24, 2026 in Dubai, United Arab Emirates.

The conference program will include:

  • Research paper presentations
  • Keynote speeches
  • Panel discussions
  • Poster and demonstration sessions
  • Workshops and tutorials
  • PhD Symposium
  • Social events

A detailed program with specific sessions, times, and locations will be published in March 2026.
