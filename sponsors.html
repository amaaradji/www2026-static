Sponsorship Opportunities

The Web Conference 2026 offers various sponsorship opportunities for organizations interested in supporting the conference and gaining visibility in the web research and development community.

Why Sponsor The Web Conference?

  • Connect with leading researchers, developers, and practitioners in web technologies
  • Showcase your organization's products, services, and innovations
  • Recruit top talent from around the world
  • Demonstrate your organization's commitment to advancing web technologies
  • Support the premier international forum for discussion about the World Wide Web

Sponsorship Packages

Platinum Sponsor - $50,000

  • Prominent logo placement on conference website, program, and signage
  • Exhibition space in prime location
  • 8 complimentary conference registrations
  • Full-page advertisement in conference program
  • Opportunity to include promotional materials in conference bags
  • Recognition during opening and closing ceremonies
  • Special mention in press releases and social media

Gold Sponsor - $30,000

  • Logo placement on conference website, program, and signage
  • Exhibition space
  • 5 complimentary conference registrations
  • Half-page advertisement in conference program
  • Opportunity to include promotional materials in conference bags
  • Recognition during opening ceremony
  • Mention in press releases and social media

Silver Sponsor - $15,000

  • Logo placement on conference website, program, and signage
  • Exhibition space
  • 3 complimentary conference registrations
  • Quarter-page advertisement in conference program
  • Recognition during opening ceremony

Bronze Sponsor - $7,500

  • Logo placement on conference website, program, and signage
  • 1 complimentary conference registration
  • Recognition during opening ceremony
